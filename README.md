# About Nyumbani Spaces

### Our Story
'Nyumbani' means *“at home”* in Swahili, and that feeling is the foundation of our existence.  

Founded by passionate real estate professionals and design enthusiasts, **Nyumbani Spaces** was created to celebrate the unique beauty of Kenyan architecture and connect people with spaces they'll love. Our mission is simple: **to make the journey of finding a property in Kenya seamless, inspiring, and enjoyable.**

---

### Our Core Values

- **Trust & Integrity**  
  We build relationships on honesty and transparency, ensuring your journey is secure every step of the way.  

- **Curated Quality**

- **Community Focus**
